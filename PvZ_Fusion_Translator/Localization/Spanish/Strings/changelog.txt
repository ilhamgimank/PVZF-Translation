<align=center><size=125%>Plantas Contra Zombis: Fusion - ¿ Qué añade de nuevo la 3.6 ?</size></align>

1. Modo Odisea Remasterizado
 - Ahora eres capaz de seleccionar libremente rutas de minijefes, dificultad, y progresión de escenarios, entregando grande flexibilidad.
 - Ya no verás modificadores que se repiten cada vez que haces una repetición, excepcionando si los tienes todos ya.
 - Se ha modificado el momento para la primera tienda de modificadores y la selección de minijefes. Ahora verás la primera tienda de modificadores después de la ronda 5. En la dificultad purgatorio y superiores, ahora tendrás que seleccionar 6 minijefes en lugar de los 4 anteriores.
 - Nuevas rutas de minijefes añadidas, estas siendo para Live Aid Jackson y Lethal-Star Abanderado, con una selección distinta de zombis por ronda y modificadores zombi exclusivos.
 - Zombistein Abismal y Arca del Patito (con Zombidito) son seleccionables como minijefes fuera de sus rutas.
 - Aún puedes acceder niveles antes de la 3.5 presionando el botón de "Preajustes".

5. Nuevos Niveles
- Minijuegos:
  - Zombie Sorter: Adjust arrows to guide zombies to the correct stations. These Signpost Arrows can also be used to create tower defense levels in Super Level Editor.
  - I, Zomboss?: Placing zombie packets will spawn a corresponding Endoflame that keeps summoning hypnotized zombies to fight for you.
  - Explode-O-su!: Control the tempo to deal with zombie waves.
- Starbound Adventure: Snow updated
- I, Zombie Adventure: Chapter 5: All Roads Lead to Roof updated
- New Odyssey level: Stats vs Mechanics: Odyssey plants disabled. Base stats of plants are greatly increased. Some seed packets are available directly in slots for quick deployment. Each time zombies reach the house, you will not immediately game over, but will be rewinded back to the last checkpoint and lose all zombie and plant modifiers. Rewinding can be only be used a limited number of times.
- Reworked Odyssey Adventure
- Garden Defense entrance moved to Zen Garden
- Harvest Mode: New Chaos difficulty. Seeds do not use durability in the difficulty but zombies gain random buffs.
- New Map: Snowy Night: Within the quiet camp, a hidden past seems to lie buried… Fertilizer does not spawn automatically on this map.

6. New Synergy
- When Litter-pult and SP Gatling Icicle-shroom are planted on the same tile, the latter's projectiles split one additional time. Both plants' normal and special projectiles inherit some properties from each other's shots. (WARNING: Will cause lag)

7. New Super Level Editor Settings
- Super Level Editor now allows creation, modification, and linking of nodes to define Level Events through a pseudo-code system.
- You can now create Rhythm Game-style levels and Tower Defense levels using Signpost Arrows.

8. Odyssey Balance Adjustments
- Apeacalypse Minigun: Reworked abilities completely
- Laser Pumpkin: Now gains the ability to reduce Zombie DR value.
- Clown-on-the-Jetski: Explosion damage reduced to 500

9. Almanac Reworks
- New Game Mechanics Almanac
- Removed Old Almanac
- Unseen plants & fusions are now locked in the almanac. Clicking on undiscovered plants now gives clues to how to make them. You can also quickly unlock all in the settings.
