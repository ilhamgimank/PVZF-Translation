<align=center><size=130%>Plants VS. Zombies: Fusion - Update 3.6 Changelog</size></align>

1. Odyssey Mode Revamp
 - You are now able to freely choose combinations of Mini-boss routes, difficulty, and stage progression, offering much greater flexibility.
 - You will no longer see repeat Modifiers on reroll, unless you have already seen all of them.
 - Timing for the first Modifier Shop and Mini-boss selection changed. You now see the first Modifier Shop after round 5. On Purgatory difficulty and above, you will now have to select 6 Mini-bosses instead of the previous 4.
 - Added new Mini-boss routes for Giga Football Striker and Jackson Worldwide, with different selection of zombie wave compositions and exclusive zombie modifiers.
 - Abyssal Gargantuar and Imperor's Ark of the Duck are now selectable Mini-bosses outside of their routes.
 - You can still access Odyssey levels pre-3.5 with the "Preset" button in the bottom right.

2. New Plants
- Base Plant:
  - Hoarfrost Lichen
- Basic Fusions:
  - Stenchwood (Torchwood + Garlic)
  - Splash (Squash > Sea-shroom)
  - Saguaro-shroom (Fume-shroom + Cactus)
  - Hypnowood (Hypno-shroom + Torchwood)
  - Grapple Kelp (Fume-shroom + Tangle Kelp)
  - Kimchi-pult (Jalapeno + Cabbage-pult)
  - Damage-pult (Doom-shroom + Cabbage-pult)
  - Popcorn-pult (Jalapeno + Kernel-pult)
  * (Note: the original popcorn-pult, Kernel-pult > Puff-shroom has been renamed to Baby Corn)
  - Ice Mine (Potato Mine + Ice-shroom)
- Odyssey Fusions:
  - Common:
    - SP Gatling Icicle-shroom (Peashooter + Gatling Icicle-shroom)
    - SP Gatling Sunburst-shroom (Peashooter + Gatling Sunburst-shroom)
  - Epic:
    - Buckshot Commando (Buckshooter + Football Gatling)
    - Synchronizer Snipea (Buckshot Commando + Chrono Core)
    - Axion Orbiter (Garlic + Photon Splitter)
    - Habanero Weeper (Cryolina Reaper > Puff-shroom)
- Legendary Fusions:
  - Eclipse Fumigator
  - Primal Twin Solar-nut
- Special/Misc:
  - Signpost Arrow
  - Puff-shroom spreads via Fume-shroom & Scaredy-shroom now have their separate IDs

5. New Levels
- Mini-games:
  - Zombie Sorter: Adjust arrows to guide zombies to the correct stations. These Signpost Arrows can also be used to create tower defense levels in Super Level Editor.
  - I, Zomboss?: Placing zombie packets will spawn a corresponding Endoflame that keeps summoning hypnotized zombies to fight for you.
  - Explode-O-su!: Control the tempo to deal with zombie waves.
- Starbound Adventure: Snow updated
- 

6. New Synergy
- Photon Splitter can lock onto any Atomheart Plantern ahead (click to select) to charge it. When energy exceeds 10k, its brightness increases, dealing heavy damage and slowing zombies in its radius. At 100k, collapses into a black hole and explodes, devastating all zombies on screen. (WARNING: Contains flashing lights)

7. Gacha Modifiers
- Gacha levels now have various exclusive modifiers that can be earned from random events.

8. Odyssey Balance Adjustments
- Apeacalypse Railgun: No longer automatically targets Atomheart Planterns in its range; instead, you must now manually click on it to target either an Atomheart or Magnate Plantern for their effects. When Railgun's laser passes through an Atomheart Plantern, it now slows zombies down and splashes small Doom-shroom explosions on the zombies it hits.
- Grim Arbiter: Now generates a Phantom Clone of the zombie with the highest health in the tile in front of it, which shares the damage it takes with all zombies of the same type on the lawn.
  - Grim Arbiter's second modifier, related to repeating on-death effects, has been replaced with unlocking its subspecies (Wicked Undertaker) and doubling the damage effect of the Phantom Clone it spawns.
- Thunder Bunnyon: Thunder Bunnyon's lightning now deals damage to any zombie that comes in contact with it and can decrease their Damage Reduction value.

9. Save File Menu
- The Achievements menu has been replaced with a menu that allows you to create new save file slots.

10. New "True Endless" Setting
- Enabling this mode will skip the cutscenes between rounds while playing Endless modes and grant a short period of time to adjust the plants you have currently selected in between rounds.

11. Miscellaneous bug fixes and optimizations.
